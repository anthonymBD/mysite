My client case study.

Please be aware that we make every effort to maintain up-to-date content. However, certain clients have specifically requested that we display images exclusively, without showcasing their website or marketing campaign..

"While working at Tylt, I quickly identified a significant challenge the company faced in distinguishing itself from its parent brand, Technocel. Tylt shared similar branding and a user base, and it struggled to grasp the American market. In fact, their competitor, Belkin, was selling identical units at a higher price point. Additionally, Tylt's old website was a duplicate of Technocel's, making it slow, non-mobile-friendly, and outdated. Through the redesign of a user-friendly website, I played a pivotal role in bridging this gap. As a result, the relaunched website led to a 25% increase in Tylt's sales."
“Before working at Thomas Jane they added every shopify plugin that shopify has to offer, sometimes known in the industry as shiny object syndrome. This not only slowed down the website to a crawl but added mixed messing across the website and brand. I helped Thomas Jane in a user experience session to clarify brand goals, have a defined voice, and grow revenue by 20% all while increasing web speed and getting rid of all the bloated code apps tend to leave. First I helped clean up the user experience with clear hierarchical messaging and helped build the email list from 200 to over 3,000. Which helped bring the conversion rate up by 45% in the first 3 months”
